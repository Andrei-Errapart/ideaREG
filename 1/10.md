### Title
Museum of Russian war crimes

### Description
The new Museum should have all kinds of documentation of Russian war crimes for the duration of the existence of Russian and/or Soviet state.

### Domain or field
Education of population

### Importance
Forgetting about these ongoing crimes is enabling people and governments to stay on friendly terms with those whose army is continuing unjust wars, effectively undermining the case for democracy by laying the first stepping stone towards autocracy. A museum would enable anyone to break this first step being done by simply reminding everybody about the existing evidence about what this will lead into.
